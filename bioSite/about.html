Biography

Janet was born on August 12, 1944. She has built a life centered around family, creativity, and dedication. Now living in Panama City Beach, Florida, she enjoys the coastal lifestyle and spending time outdoors.

Throughout her life, Janet has demonstrated patience, resilience, and a deep love for the people around her. Her creativity shines through in everything she does, especially in the hobbies she has developed over the years.

Traveling with my grandfather is one of her favorite experiences. Whether exploring new places or enjoying familiar destinations, she values the memories created along the way.
